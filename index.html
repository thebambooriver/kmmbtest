About Us


Koperasi Malayalee Malaysia Berhad was officially registered in 2010.

It was set up as a hybrid co-operative, banding together the common interests of the Malaysian Malayalee community, to look into areas of investment as well as to support small business.

In 2019, KMMB embarked on a rebranding and is now re-launched as an investment co-operative which functions as a community based socio-economic platform. Its main purpose to deliver long-term sustainable returns to its members annually.
